Definition 1.1.2.

In general, we can define scalar multiplication of a vector as
\begin{equation} c\mathbf{v} = c\cdot \begin{bmatrix} v_1\\ v_2\\ \vdots\\ v_n \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} cv_1\\ cv_2\\ \vdots\\ cv_n \end{bmatrix}\tag{1.1.1} \end{equation}
where \(c\in \mathbb{R}\text{.}\)
